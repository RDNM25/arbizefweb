// FAQ.EXE

Common questions.

How long does a typical project take?

It depends on scope. A landing page can be done in 1–2 weeks. A full web app or game can take 2–4 months. We'll give you a clear timeline after our first conversation.

Do you work with clients remotely?

100%. We're a remote-first team and have worked with clients across different countries. We use async tools and weekly video check-ins to keep things smooth.

What's your payment structure?

We typically work with a 50% upfront deposit and 50% on delivery. For larger projects, we can split into milestones. We accept bank transfer, PayPal, and crypto.

Can you maintain the project after launch?

Yes. We offer retainer-based maintenance packages for hosting, updates, and new features. Most clients stick around — which we think says a lot.

I just have an idea — is that enough to reach out?

Absolutely. Some of our best projects started as a rough napkin sketch. Come as you are — we're good at helping shape ideas into something real.
